NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES ACT, 1994
IN RESPECT OF SPECIAL RESOLUTION TO WIND UP VOLUNTARY
In the matter of:
EARTH SCIENCE TECHNOLOGY AND POLICY SERVICES LIMITED
Reg. No. C-147747
An Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM) of the members of EARTH
SCIENCE TECHNOLOGY AND POLICY SERVICES LIMITED duly
convened and held at the Registered Office of the company at Amina Vista,
Flat-5C, 30 Topkhana Road, Shahabag, Dhaka-1000 on 05-07-2026. It has been
resolved that EARTH SCIENCE TECHNOLOGY AND POLICY SERVICES
LIMITED has been put into Member voluntary Winding-up on 05-07-2026. Dr.
Dewan Abdul quadir, Managing Director of EARTH SCIENCE
TECHNOLOGY AND POLICY SERVICES LIMITED of Amina Vista, Flat-
5C, 30 Topkhana Road, Shahabag, Dhaka-1000 has been appointed as Liquidator
of the Company.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the company.
DR. DEWAN ABDUL QUADIR
Managing Director.
Liquidator of the Company.
