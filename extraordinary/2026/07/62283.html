NOTICE
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES AСТ, 1994
FOR RESOLUTION TO WINDING UP VOLUNTARILY
Date: 06-06-2026
In the matter of
DIGITAL MOTORS LIMITED
An extraordinary general meeting of the shareholders of Digital Motors Limited, Reg.
No. C-136091, Reg. Date: 19 FEB-2017 duly convened on Saturday, 28th June, 2026 and
held at Wakil Tower (10th Floor), TA-131, Gulshan Badda Link Road, Dhaka, Bangladesh
the registered office of the Company at 11 a.m. the following special resolutions has been
resolved that:
01. That the Company "Digital Motors Limited, Reg. No. C-136091, Reg. Date:
19-FEB-2017" be put into Members Voluntary Winding Up under the provisions of
the Companies Act, 1994 effective from 28th June, 2026.
02. That the Sanjida Hoque, Managing Director of the Company, House-652, Road-14,
Baitul Aman Housing Society, Adabor, Mohammadpur, Dhaka-1207 be and hereby
appointed as liquidator of the Company as recommended by the Board of
Directors to initiate & complete winding up process under the provisions of the
companies Act, 1994.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator in
respect of any affairs of the company.
SANJIDA HOQUE
Managing Director
Digital Motors Limited.
