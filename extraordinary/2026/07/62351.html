NOTICE
A meeting of M/s. Aim Soureing House duly convened and held at the
Registered office of the partnership Firm at 218/4, West Kafrul, Begum Rokeya
Sarani, Kafrul, Dhaka-1206 on 16-05-2026 at 10.00 A.M. It has been resolved
that M/s. Aim Soureing House Reg. No. P-54828, Date: 18-03-2025 has been
put into Partners for voluntary Dissolution on 16-05-2026. ARIFUL HAQUE as
Managing Partner, EKRAMUL ALAM as Partner of the Firm were present in
the Meeting.

All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the ARIFUL
HAQUE, Managing Partner in respect of any affairs of the Firm.

ARIFUL HAQUE
Managing Partner.
