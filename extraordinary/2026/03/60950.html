NOTICE

An extraordinary general meeting of the Shareholders of Bhangari
Manufacturing Ltd. duly convened and held at the registered office of the
Company in Dhaka on 04-03-2026 at 10.00 A.M. It has been resolved that
Bhangari Manufacturing Ltd. (Reg. No. C-113145) has been put into Members
for voluntary Winding up on 04-03-2026 Md Mokshed Ali Khan Sohel,
Chairman address: House-07, Road-18, Sector-04, Uttara East, Uttara-1230,
Dhaka, will appointment as the Liquidator of the Company.

All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the company.

S M RUMAT ASHRAF
Managing Director.
