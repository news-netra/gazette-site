NOTICE
CHAKLADER SHIPPING LINES LIMITED
[Reg. No. C-132089/2016, Reg. Date: 28-JUL-2016]
At an Extra ordinary General Meeting of the CHAKLADER SHIPPING
LINES LIMITED duly convened and held at its registered office of the
Company at 61, Bijoy Nagar Estran Arzoo Tower, (13th Floor), Room No-7-8,
Dhaka-1000 at 10.00 am on the 04th day of March, 2026. It has been resolved
that CHAKLADER SHIPPING LINES LIMITED has been put into Members
voluntary winding up Dr. Md. Jahangir Alam Chaklader, Managing Director
has been appointed as the Liquidator of the Company.
By order of the Board
DR. MD. JAHANGIR ALAM CHAKLADER
Managing Director
CHAKLADER SHIPPING LINES LIMITED.
