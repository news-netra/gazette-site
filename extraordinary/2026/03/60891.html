PUBLIC NOTICE
IN THE MATTER OF "M/S. NEO PEARL CERAMICS"
Notice regarding the reorganization of a partnership firm under Section 63 of the
Partnership Act. 1932. The notice informs that, with the addition of new
provisions in partnership deed 8 no para for bank loans as under:
"The firm shall be in power to take loan from any banking/financial institution
upon such terms & conditions as are found suitable. Even such loan may be taken
from any person firm & company if any, on such terms & conditions as the firm
finds acceptable. For re-payment of such loan, priority must be given in all times.
Provided that, the Board of Management must have approved such terms &
conditions including loan taking & payment issue too."
The partnership firm “M/S. NEO PEARL CERAMICS” has been reorganized
on 20th day of May, 2025. At present, 25%, 25%, 25% and 25% shares are held
by Rizia Begum, Md. Emdadul Haque, Mahfuza Begum and Mohammed
Towhidul Hoque Chowdhury respectively.
