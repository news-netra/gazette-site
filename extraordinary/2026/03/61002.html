PUBLIC NOTICE
IN THE MATTER OF M/S. SYED AULIA THAI ALUMINIUM
Advertisement under Section 63 of the Partnership Act, 1932
(Reconstitution of Partnership Firm)
It is hereby notified that upon the retirement of Mr. Md. Rokan & Jabal Hossan
as a partner, the Partnership Firm M/S. Syed Aulia Thai Aluminium has been
reconstituted with effect from March 1, 2026.
MD. ROKAN, S/O. Syed Md. Nurul Islam of 403, Syed Aulia Bari, Gujra,
PO-Thakur Bari-4341, Raozan, Chattogram has transferred his 17% share in the
firm in favour of Mr. Md. Delwar Hossain, S/O Syed Mohammad Nurul
Islam, & JABAL HOSSAN, S/O. Syed Md. Nurul Islam of 403, Syed Aulia
Bari, Gujra, PO-Thakur Bari-4341, Raozan, Chattogram has transferred his
16% share in the firm in favour of Mr. Md. Delwar Hossain, S/O Syed
Mohammad Nurul Islam, residing at the same address.
After such reconstitution, the partners of the firm and their respective shares are
as follows:
•
Syed Mansur Alam— 50% share
• Md. Delwar Hossain— 50% share
SYED MANSUR ALAM
মোহাম্মদ আবু ইউসুফ, উপপরিচালক (উপসচিব), বাংলাদেশ সরকারী মুদ্রণালয়, তেজগাঁও, ঢাকা কর্তৃক মুদ্রিত।
মোঃ নজরুল ইসলাম, উপপরিচালক (উপসচিব), বাংলাদেশ ফরম ও প্রকাশনা অফিস, তেজগাঁও,
ঢাকা কর্তৃক প্রকাশিত। website: www.bgpress.gov.bd
