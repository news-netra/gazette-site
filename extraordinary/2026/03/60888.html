NOTICE
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES ACT, 1994
FOR RESOLUTION TO WIND UP VOLUNTARILY
In the matter of
EXTRA INDUSTRIES LTD.
An extraordinary general meeting of the shareholders EXTRA INDUSTRIES
LTD. duly convened and held at the Registered office of the Company in Dhaka
10.00 A.M. It has been resolved that EXTRA INDUSTRIES LTD. (Reg: KHC-
2608/2024) has been put into Members for voluntary Winding up on 25-01-2026
Md. Abul Kashem, Address: House: 46, Kismat, Noapara, Beltola, Jail Road,
Jessore has been appointed as the Liquidator of the Company.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the Company.
MD. ABUL KASHEM
Managing Director.
মোহাম্মদ আবু ইউসুফ, উপপরিচালক (উপসচিব), বাংলাদেশ সরকারী মুদ্রণালয়, তেজগাঁও, ঢাকা কর্তৃক মুদ্রিত।
