PUBLIC NOTICE
IN THE MATTER OF CHIC WINGS (BD) TEXTILE CO. LTD.
Advertisement of notice under sub-section-I of Section 289 of the Companies
Act. 1994 of Resolution of Members voluntary winding up.
An Extraordinary General Meeting of the Company duly convened was held at
Registered Office at Flat # 09-C, House # 61/C, Road # 02, Khulshi Hills R/A,
Chattogram. on March 10, 2026 at 4.00 P.M. and the following resolutions were
duly passed as Special Resolutions:-
• Resolved unanimously in the meeting that the Company via CHIC
WINGS (BD) TEXTILE CO. LTD. be and is hereby wound up
voluntarily with effect from March 10, 2026.
• Resolved that MR. CHEN XI, Managing Director of CHIC WINGS
(BD) TEXTILE CO. LTD. is appointed as liquidator with power to
process all activities upon winding of the Company.
CHEN XI
Managing Director.
