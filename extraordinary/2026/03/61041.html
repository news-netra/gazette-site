NOTICE
A meeting of M/s. Capella Ship Management duly convened and held at the
Registered office of the Partnership Firm at Ka/2, Ma-Moni Bhaban, Ground
Floor, Room No-03, Khilkhet, Dhaka. It has been resolved that M/s. Capella
Ship Management, Reg. No.-P-47308, Date: 18-07-2021 has been put into
Partners for voluntary Dissolution on 10-03-2026 (1) Md. Ranakul Islam (2)
Mrs. Sharmin Sharif (3) Mr. S.M. Alamgir Hossain (4) Mr. Chowdhury Arif
Uddin Ahmed Partners were present in the Meeting.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the
Md. Ranakul Islam in respect of any affairs of the Firm.
MD. RANAKUL ISLAM
Managing Partner
M/s. Capella Ship Management
