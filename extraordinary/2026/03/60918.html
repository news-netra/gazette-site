PUBLIC NOTICE
Advertisement on notice under section 289(1) of the Companies Act, 1994 of
Resolution of wind up voluntarily.

In the Matter of Haicheng Mobile Company (Bd) Limited at an Extraordinary
General Meeting of the above named Company duly convened and held at Police
Plaza Concord, 09th Floor, Flat No. J & K, Plot No-2, Road No-144, Gulshan-
1, Dhaka-1212, Bangladesh on the February 26, 2026 at 11.00 A. M and the
following was duly passed as Special Resolution:-

• Resolved that the company viz of Haicheng Mobile Company (Bd)
Limited be and is hereby wound up voluntarily with effect from
February 26, 2026.

• Resolved that Mr. Zainul Abedin Khan, ITP, Senior Manager of
A. Qasem & Co., be and is hereby appointed Liquidator for the purpose
of the winding up.

• Resolved further that Mr. Zainul Abedin Khan, ITP, Liquidator of the
company be and is hereby authorized, empowered and directed to do all
acts and things that may be necessary for the Liquidation purpose.

MR. DU BAOHUA
Managing Director.
