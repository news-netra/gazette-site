NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES ACT 1994
FOR RESOLUTION TO WINDING UP VOLUNTARILY
In the matter of
INNOV8 LTD.
An extraordinary general meeting of the shareholders of INNOV8 LTD. duly
convened and held at the registered office of the Company in Dhaka. It has been
resolved that INNOV8 LTD. Reg. No. C-200185, Reg. Date: 28-JAN-2025 has
been put into Members for voluntary Winding up on 10-03-2026. Mohammad Abu
Baker Siddik, Managing Director Address: House-20, Road-07, Sector-01, Uttara,
Dhaka-1230 has been appointed as the Liquidator of the Company.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the company.
MOHAMMAD ABU BAKER SIDDIK
Managing Director
INNOV8 LTD.
