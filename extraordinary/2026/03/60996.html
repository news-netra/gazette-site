NOTICE UNDER PARTNERSHIP ACT 1932
FOR RESOLUTION TO DISSOLUTION VOLUNTARILY

In the matter of
M/S. THE LABIBA OVERSEAS

A Meeting of M/S. THE LABIBA OVERSEAS duly convened and held at the
registered office of the Partnership Firm in Dhaka on 30-10-2025 at 11.00 A.M.
It has been resolved that M/S. THE LABIBA OVERSEAS, P-52119/2023,
Date: 03-12-2023 has been put into Partners for voluntary Dissolution on
30-10-2025.

All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Partner in
respect of any affairs of the Firm.
