NOTICE
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289(1) OF THE COMPANIES ACT 1994
FOR RESOLUTION TO WINDING UP VOLUNTARILY

In the matter of
UKBANGLA IT LIMITED

An extraordinary general meeting of the shareholders of UKBANGLA IT
LIMITED duly convened and held on March 10, 2026 at 11.00 A.M. at the
registered office of the Company at House- # 340 (1st Floor), Road # 03, Baitul
Aman Housing Society, Adabor, Mirpur, Dhaka, Bangladesh and It has been
resolved that UKBANGLA IT LIMITED Reg. No. C-137173, Reg. Date:
11-APR-17 has been put into Members for voluntary Winding up on 10-03-2026.
Mr. Koushick Ahmad, Managing Director Address: House # 340 (1st Floor) Road #
03, Baitul Aman Housing Society, Adabor Mirpur, Dhaka, Bangladesh has been
appointed as the Liquidator of the Company.

All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the company.

KOUSHICK AHMAD
Managing Director.
