PUBLIC NOTICE

IN THE MATTER OF YUANCHANG STORE (BD) LIMITED

Advertisement of notice under sub-section-I of Section 289 of the Companies
Act, 1994 of Resolution of Members voluntary winding up.

An Extraordinary General Meeting of the Company duly convened was held at
Registered Office at 25/Sami Flora Pass Road, South Khulshi, Chattogram on
March 10, 2026 at 4.00 P.M and the following resolutions were duly passed as
Special Resolutions:—

• Resolved unanimously in the meeting that the Company via
YUANCHANG STORE (BD) LIMITED be and is hereby wound up
voluntarily with effect from March 10, 2026.
• Resolved that MR. XIAO HONG BIN, Managing Director of
YUANCHANG STORE (BD) LIMITED is appointed as liquidator
with power to process all activities upon winding of the Company.
