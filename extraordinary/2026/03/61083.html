Notice Under section 289 of the companies act, 1994
for resolution to wind up voluntarily
In the matter of
AM AUTOMOBILES LTD.
An extraordinary general meeting of the shareholders AM AUTOMOBILES
LTD. duly convened and held at the Registered office of the Company in Dhaka
on 04-03-2026 at 10.00 A.M. It has been resolved that AM AUTOMOBILES
LTD. (Reg. No. C-150779, Reg. Date: 20-MAR-19) has been put into Members
for voluntary Winding up on 04-03-2026 MD. MIZANUR RAHMAN, Managing
Director, AM AUTOMOBILES LTD. Address: Ka-71, Nadda Progati Sarani,
Vatara, Dhaka-1212 has been appointed as the Liquidator of the Company with
effect from 04-03-2026.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the company.
