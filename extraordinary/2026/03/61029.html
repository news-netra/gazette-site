PUBLIC NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES ACT, 1994
FOR VOLUNTARY WINDING UP OF
DIRECT SOURCE INDUSTRIES BANGLADESH PVT. LTD.
At an Extraordinary general meeting of Direct Source Industries Bangladesh Pvt.
Ltd. duly convened on 10 March, 2026 and held at Amanullah, Trade Center,
Building 26, Gulshan Avenue, Dhaka-1212, Dhaka at 11:00 A.M the following
special resolutions were duly passed.
1.
That the company be wound-up voluntarily.
2.
That Mr. Md. Saydur Rahman FCA, address: Plot # 3D Kabbakos
Super Market, 8th Floor, Room No. 8/11, 8/12, 8/13, 8/14
Kawranbazar, Dhaka 1215, Bangladesh be and is hereby appointed as
liquidator of the company as recommended by the directors to initiate
& complete winding up process under the provisions of the companies
Act 1994.
VENU GOPAL NAMBIAR
Director.
মোহাম্মদ আবু ইউসুফ, উপপরিচালক (উপসচিব), বাংলাদেশ সরকারী মুদ্রণালয়, তেজগাঁও, ঢাকা কর্তৃক মুদ্রিত।
