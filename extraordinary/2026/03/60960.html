NOTICE UNDER PARTNERSHIP ACT 1932 FOR RESOLUTION ΤΟ
DISSOLUTION VOLUNTARILY
In the matter of
M/S. LP Fashion
A meeting of M/s. LP Fashion duly convened and held at the Registered office
of the Partnership Firm at 31/147, Chandal Vog, Turag, Dhaka-1230 on
02-03-2026 at 10.00 A.M. (1) Litan Sikder, Managing Partner (2) Puja Sikder as
partner of the Firm were present in the meeting. It has been resolved that
M/s. L P Fashion P-34061, Date: 09-08-2011 has been put into Partners for
voluntary Dissolution on 02-03-2026.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Managing
Partner in respect of any affairs of the Firm.
LITAN SIKDER
Managing Partner
M/s. L P Fashion
