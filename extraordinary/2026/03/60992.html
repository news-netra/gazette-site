NOTICE UNDER PARTNERSHIP ACT 1932 FOR RESOLUTION TO
DISSOLUTION VOLUNTARILY

In the matter of
M/S. D P L Fashion

A meeting of M/s. D P L Fashion duly convened and held at the Registered
office of the Partnership Firm at 31/147, Chandal Vog, Turag, Dhaka-1230 on
02-03-2026 at 10.00 A.M. (1) Litan Sikder, Managing Partner (2) Dr. Dulal Shikder,
Partner (3) Palash Kumar Raha, Partner (4) Puja Sikder, Partner as partner
of the Firm were present in the meeting. It has been resolved that M/s. D P L
Fashion P-33671, Date: 18-01-2011 has been put into Partners for voluntary
Dissolution on 02-03-2026.

All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Managing
Partner in respect of any affairs of the Firm.

LITAN SIKDER
Managing Partner
M/s. DPL Fashion.
