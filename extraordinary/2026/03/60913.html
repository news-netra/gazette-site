NOTICE OF THE EXTRA-ORDINARY GENERAL MEETING
Notice is hereby given that the Extra-ordinary General Meeting of the
shareholders of KINSAN ENTERPRISE LTD will be held at the registered
office, 9 No Boro Katara Chawk Bazar, Dhaka, On Date 30-03-2026 at 11.00
a.m. to transact the following business:
1.
To read and confirm the minutes of the last Meeting.
2.
To consider the proposal to put the company into members Voluntary
Winding up and to consider the declaration of solvency by the directors of
the company and to adopt necessary resolution.
3.
Miscellaneous, if any, with the permission of the chair.
By order of the Board
WAHEDUZ JAMAN ADNAN
Managing Director.
