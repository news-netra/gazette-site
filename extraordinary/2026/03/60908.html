NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF RESOLUTION
ΤΟ WIND UP VOLUNTARILY

In the matter of Farmvit Limited

At An extraordinary general meeting of the shareholders of Farmvit limited duly
convened and held at Registered Office of the company on the 2nd June, 2024. It
has been resolved that Farmvit limited has been put into members for voluntarily
winding up from 2nd June, 2024 and Md. Towhidul Islam been appointed as
Liquidator of Farmvit limited.

MD. TOWHIDUL ISLAM
Liquidator
Farmvit Limited.
