Notice Under section 289 of the companies act 1994
for resolution to wind up voluntarily

In the matter of
Enterprise Tech Limited

House# 37, Road # 11, Block # H, Banani, Dhaka-1213 of the company in
Dhaka on 28-02-2026 at 10:00 A .M. It has been resolved that Enterprise Tech
Limited, Reg. No. C-197035, Reg. Date: 04-SEP-24, has been put into
Members for voluntary Winding up on 28-02-2026, MD. MERAZUL ISLAM,
ITP. Address: 57, Purana Paltan Line, SEl Trident Tower, (9th Floor), Dhaka-
1000 has been appointed as the liquidator of the Company with effect from
28-02-2026.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the company.
