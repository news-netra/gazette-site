NOTICE
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES ACT 1994
FOR RESOLUTION TO WIND UP VOLUNTARILY
In the matter of
GREEN POLYTEX LIMITED.
An extraordinary general meeting of the shareholders of GREEN POLYTEX
LIMITED duly convened and held at the registered office of the Company in
Dhaka on 04-03-2026 at 10.00 A.M. It has been resolved that GREEN
POLYTEX LIMITED has been put into Members for voluntary Winding up
Md. Hayder Harun Ur Rashid Khan, FCS, Address: House: 24, Road: 20, Block-K,
Banani, Dhaka-1212. Has been appointed as the Liquidator of the Company.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the Company.
MD. TARIQUL ISLAM
Managing Director
GREEN POLYTEX LIMITED.
