Date: March 10, 2026
Advertisement of Notice of Under Section 298(1) the Final Extra
Ordinary General Meeting to Liquidation.
A notice is hereby given that Final Extra Ordinary General Meeting of the
company Akota Trade and Business Ltd. will be held at the registered office at
Baliadanga (Ghontola), P.O: Baliadanga, Chapainawabganj Sadar, Chapainawabganj of
the Company on the 20th day of April, 2026 at 10.00 a.m. It has been resolved
that Akota Trade and Business Ltd. has been put into member voluntary
Winding up and Mr. Md. Abu Bakker to appointed as the Liquidator of the
company to transact the following purpose:
(1) Resolved that the final liquidator's Report and audited accounts will be
adopted and approved.
(2) Resolved that Akota Trade and Business Ltd. will be finally liquidated
and the liquidator will be discharged from 20th day of April, 2026.
(3) Miscellaneous.
MR. MD. ABU BAKKER
Liquidator/Managing Director.
