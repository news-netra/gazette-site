NOTICE OF EXTRA-ORDINARY GENERAL MEETING FOR
LIQUIDATION
OF
AMINOVIT BANGLADESH PRIVATE LIMITED
Notice is hereby given to all shareholders of Aminovit Bangladesh Private
Limited that an Extraordinary General Meeting will be held on 9th April 2026 at
11.00 A.M. at Holding-25 (6th Floor), Road No. 07, Sector-03, Uttara Model
Town, Dhaka-1230, Dhaka to consider the following Agenda.
Ordinary Resolution:
01. To adopt fraction period audited financials for the period from 1st July
2024 to 20th January 2025.
“RESOLVED THAT the Balance Sheet of the Company as on 20th
January 2025 with the notes to the Auditor's Financial Statements for the
period from 1st July 2024 to 20th January 2025 along with Auditor's report
thereon be and is hereby approved and adopted."
02. To receive and consider the liquidator's report and accounts prepared
by liquidator.
"RESOLVED THAT the report and accounts presented by liquidator be &
is hereby accepted and approved.”
(১৩৭৪৩)
মূল্য: টাকা ৪.০০
১৩৭৪৪
বাংলাদেশ গেজেট, অতিরিক্ত, মার্চ ১০, ২০২৬
03. To discharge the liquidator.
“RESOLVED THAT the liquidator be discharged from his duties and
responsibilities in line with relevant provisions of concerned laws of
Bangladesh."
Special Resolution:
01. To wind up the company finally.
“RESOLVED THAT the Company finally be wound up as there are no
claims from any party and the liquidator is paid his remuneration and
balance amount will be returned to the members of the company".
All members are requested to attend the meeting.
MD. SAYDUR RAHAMAN FCA
Liquidator.
