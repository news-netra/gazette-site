LEGAL NOTICE
This is to inform the general public that M/s. SHUBHANKAR & CO.
CHARTERED ACCOUNTANTS,. a partnership firm registered under the
Partnership Act, 1932 (Registration No.: P-53102, Date: 04-JUNE-24), having its
principal office at Suite-1005, SEL Trident Tower, 57 Purana Paltan Line, VIP
Road, Dhaka-1000, has undergone the following changes:
1. Change of Firm Name:
The name of the frim has been changed from M/S. SHUBHANKAR & CO.
CHARTERED ACCOUNTANTS to JKMNS CHARTERED ACCOUNTANTS,
subject to approval by the Registrar of Joint Stock Companics & Firms (RJSC),
Bangladesh.
2. Admission of New Partners:
Md. Mijanur Rahman, FCA and Jagadish Chandra Biswas, FCA have been
admitted as partners with effect from 01 January 2026.
(১৪৪৮৫)

3. Reallocation of Partnership Shares:
The partners have mutually agreed to revise the capital contribution and profit-
sharing ratio as follows:
• Shubhankar Shil, FCA transfer to Md. Mijanur Rahman, FCA—13.333%
• Khokan Chandra Das, FCA transfer to Md. Mijanur Rahman, FCA—6.665%
• Khokan Chandra Das, FCA transfer to Jagadish Chandra Biswas, FCA—6.665%
• Nirmal Roy, FCA transfer to Jagadish Chandra Biswas, FCA—13.333%
4. Execution of Reconstitution Deed:
A Deed of Reconstitution of partnership has been executed to refiect the above
changes, effective from 01 January 2026
All concerned persons are hereby notified that henceforth all business
transactions, liabilities, and activities of the firm shall be conducted in the name
of JKMNS CHARTERED ACCOUNTANTS. Any objection, if any, should be
communicated in writing within thirty (30) days from the date of publication of
this notice.
