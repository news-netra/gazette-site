A notice is hereby given that Final Extraordinary General Meeting of the company
ASTER Laboratory Ayurvedic Ltd. will be held at the registered office at 27,
Moulavirtak, Khilgaon, Taltola, Dhaka-1219 of the Company on the 20th day of May,
2026 at 10.00 a.m. It has been resolved that ASTER Laboratory Ayurvedic Ltd. has
been put into member voluntary winding up and Mr. Md. Fazlul Haque to appointed as
the Liquidator of the company to transact the following purpose.
(1) Resolved that the final liquidator's Report and audited accounts will be
adopted and approved.
(2) Resolved that ASTER Laboratory Ayurvedic Ltd. will be finally liquidated
and the liquidator will be discharged from 20th day of May, 2026.
(3) Miscellaneous.

Distribution to:
1. Mr. Md. Fazlul Haque
2. Mazbah Uddin Bhuiyen
3. Md. Golam Hakkani
4. A.K.M. Jashim Uddin
5. Ferdosi Easmin
