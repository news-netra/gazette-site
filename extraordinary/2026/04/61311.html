NOTICE OF FINAL ORDINARY GENERAL MEETING OF
SMS MOBILE LIMITED FOR COMPANY LIQUIDATION
Notice is hereby given that the final ordinary general meeting of SMS MOBILE
LIMITED will be held on 29th day of April 2026 at 11:00 am at the registered
office of the company at BSEC Bhaban (Level-11). 102 Kazi Nazrul Islam
Avenue, Kawran Bazar, Dhaka, for the following purpose:
1. To receive and consider the liquidator's final report and accounts prepared by
her in accordance with the section 296 and the Company Liquidated as per
Companies Act 1994.
2. To transact any other business that the liquidator may place before the
meeting.
All the members of the company are requested to attend the meeting in time.
(RICARDO GABRIEL VARELA)
Liquidator.
