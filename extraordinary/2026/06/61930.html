NOTICE OF THE SOCIETY ACT, 1860
FOR THE SPECIAL RESOLUTION TO VOLUNTARY WINDING UP
In the matter of
HEAL BANGLADESH FOUNDATION
At an Extraordinary General meeting of Executive Member of HEAL BANGLADESH
FOUNDATION duly convened and held at the Registered office of the Society at
House-1A/2, Road-07, Gulshan, Dhaka-1212 at 10:00 A.M. on the 03rd June-2026.
It has been resolved that (1) HEAL BANGLADESH FOUNDATION Reg. No.
S-12842, Reg. Date: 10-May-2018 has been put into Members voluntary
winding up and (2) SHAHEEN ISLAM, Address: Flat-M-2 Baily Ritz Appt., 1
New Baily Road, Dhaka-1000 Dhaka of the Society for the purpose of voluntary
winding up.
All concerned are hereby requested to contact henceforth with any affairs of the
Society.
SHAHEEN ISLAM
Chairman
HEAL BANGLADESH FOUNDATION.
