NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES ACT 1994 FOR
RESOLUTION TO WIND UP VOLUNTARILY

In the matter of
JANG ON DORY FASHION (PVT.) LTD.

An extraordinary general meeting of the shareholders of JANG ON DORY
FASHION (PVT.) LTD. duly convened and held at the registered office of the
Company in Dhaka at 10:00 A.M. It has been resolved JANG ON DORY
FASHION (PVT.) LTD., Reg. No. C-70161, Reg. Date: 03-Mar-2008 has
been put into Members for voluntary Winding up on: 10-06-2026 S. M.
HOSSAIN, Address: House-45, Road-01, Block-E, Bansree, Rampura, Dhaka-
1219 has been appointed as the Liquidator of the Company.

All concerned are therefore, requested to attend the EGM and to contact
henceforth with the Liquidator in respect of any affairs of the company.

S. M. HOSSAIN
Managing Director
JANG ON DORY FASHION (PVT.) LTD.
