NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES ACT, 1994
FOR RESOLUTION TO WIND UP VOLUNTARILY

In the matter of
SECURED TRADE CENTRE LIMITED

An extraordinary general meeting of the shareholders of SECURED TRADE
CENTRE LIMITED duly convened and held at the Registered office of the
Company in Dhaka on 15-06-2026 at 10.00 A.M. It has been resolved that
SECURED TRADE CENTRE LIMITED (Registration # C-195222/2024) has
been put into Members for voluntary Winding up on 15-06-2026.
MD. MOHIUDDIN FAKIR, Managing Director, Address :307/1/B/2, Flat No-
5, 71, Tower, Jhilpar, 60 Fit, Mirpur, Dhaka has been appointed as the Liquidator
of the Company with effect from 15-06-2026.

All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the company.

MD. SAIFUL ISLAM
Chairman
SECURED TRADE CENTRE LIMITED
