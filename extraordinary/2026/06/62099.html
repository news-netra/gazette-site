NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES ACT, 1994
FOR THE SPECIAL RESOLUTION TO VOLUNTARY WINDING UP

In the matter of
BARIND HOSPITAL UNIT-2 LTD.

At an Extra ordinary General Meeting of shareholders of BARIND HOSPITAL
UNIT-2 LTD. duly convened and held at the Registered office of the Company
at S.M. Tower. House-14, Road-2, Sector-3, Uttara, Dhaka at 10.00 A.M on the
18 day of June, 2026. It has been resolved that (1) BARIND HOSPITAL
UNIT-2 LTD., Reg. No. C-163654 has been put into Members voluntary
winding up and (2) Advocate Tapash Rajbongshi, of 33 Kawran Bazar, Shah
Ali (4th floor), Dhaka has been appointed as the Liquidator of the Company for
the purpose of voluntary winding up.
