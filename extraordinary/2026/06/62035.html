NOTICE
OF FINAL EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL MEETING
(MEMBERS' VOLUNTARY WINDING UP) OF
IKD ENGINEERING LTD.

Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to the provisions of the Companies Act,
1994, the Final Extraordinary General Meeting of the Members of IKD
Engineering Ltd. (In Members' Voluntary Winding up) will be held at the
Registered Office of the Company at Apt-B204, House No. 11/B, Road No. 71,
Gulshan-2, Dhaka, on 20-07-2026 at 10:00 a.m. for the purpose of laying before the
Members the final accounts of the winding-up and giving an explanation thereof.

Agenda to be discussed:

01. To confirm the minutes of the previous meeting.

02. To receive, consider, and adopt the Liquidator's Final Statement of
Accounts showing the manner in which the winding-up has been
conducted and the Company's assets have been disposed of.

(১৯৩৯৫)
মূল্য : টাকা ৪.০০

03. To consider and approve the final distribution of assets (if any) and
settlement of liabilities among the Members in accordance with law.

04. To pass necessary resolutions for the dissolution of the Company and to
authorize the Liquidator to file the required returns with the Registrar of
Joint Stock Companies and Firms (RJSC).

05. Miscellaneous (If Any).

House-80, Rd-2, Block-F, Banani, Dhaka.
