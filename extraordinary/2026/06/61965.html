Notice of Extraordinary General Meeting For Liquidation of TLSContact
Bangladesh Ltd.

Notice is hereby given to all shareholders and members of TLSContact
Bangladesh Ltd. that an Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM) will be held
under Section 296 of the Companies Act, 1994.

• Date: 08-07-2026 (July 8, 2026)
• Time: 3:00 PM
• Venue: Registered office of the Company
Anamicka Concord, 11-F, 583 West Shewrapara, Miupur-12, Dhaka-
1216, Bangladesh

Agenda:
To consider, review, and approve the Liquidator's Report and final accounts,
along with any explanations relating thereto, as prepared following the
completion of the company's winding-up process in accordance with the
provisions of the Act.

All shareholders are earnestly requested to attend the meeting on time.

DR. AMINA KHATOON, Advocate
Liquidator
House-153/2, Road 2/2, Block A,
Section 12, Mirpur, Dhaka-1216.
