ADVERTISEMENT OF NOTICE OF UNDER SECTION 298(1) THE
FINAL EXTRA
ORDINARY GENERAL MEETING TO LIQUIDATION.
A notice is hereby given that Final Extraordinary General Meeting of the
company RIMSHAD TEX LTD. (Reg. No. C-54552) will be held at the
Registered office at House-124, Lane-5, D.O.H.S Mohakhali, Dhaka of the
Company on the 30th Day of March 2026 at 2.00 p. m. It has been resolved that
RIMSHAD TEX LTD. has been put into member voluntary Winding up and
Advocate S.M. Abul Kashem, Liquidator, of Townhall Govt. Market, Medicine
Lane, (Ground floor), Mohammadpur, Dhaka-1207, to transact the following
purpose:
1. Resolved that the final Liquidator's Report and audited accounts will be
adopted and approved.
2. Resolved that RIMSHAD TEX LTD. will be finally liquidated and the
liquidator will be discharged from 30th day of March, 2026.
3. Miscellaneous.
ADVOCATE S.M. ABUL KASHEM
Liquidator.
