NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES ACT, 1994
IN RESPECT OF SPECIAL RESOLUTION TO WIND UP VOLUNTARY
In the matter of
The Forest Lounge Ltd., Reg. No. C-157923
An Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM) of the members of The Forest
Lounge Ltd., Reg. No. C-157923 duly convened and held at the Registered
Office of the company at House No. 46, Bojohori Saha Street, Wari (Flat-A8),
Dhaka on 10-02-2026, It has been resolved that The Forest Lounge Ltd.,
Reg. No. C-157923 has been put into member's voluntary Winding-up on
10-02-2026. Mr. Nuruddin Ahmed, Managing Director of House No. 46,
Bojohori Saha Street, Wari (Flat-A8), Dhaka, has been appointed as Liquidator
of the Company.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the Company.
MR. NURUDDIN AHMED
Managing Director of The Forest Lounge Ltd.
Liquidator of the Company.
মোহাম্মদ আবু ইউসুফ, উপপরিচালক (উপসচিব), বাংলাদেশ সরকারী মুদ্রণালয়, তেজগাঁও, ঢাকা কর্তৃক মুদ্রিত।
