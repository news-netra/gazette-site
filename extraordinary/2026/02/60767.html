NOTICE
Dated: January 21, 2026
PURSUANT UNDER SECTION 289, COMPANIES ACT. 1994
Notice of Members Voluntary Winding-up of Master Mac Limited a Private
Limited Company with Registration No. C-174141, Dated: 16-09-2021 of
Registrar of Joint Stock Companies & Firms, Dhaka, Bangladesh.
Master Mac Limited has been wound up effective from and commencing on
February 15, 2026 by its Members Voluntary Winding-up. Mr. Md. Jasim
Uddin, Address: Village: Tarafpur, Post: Tarafpur, Thana: Mirzapur, Tangail,
Bangladesh has been appointed as the liquidator of the company for the purpose
of winding-up and distributing the net assets of the company and completing all
proceeding required under the Companies Act. 1994.
MD. ASFAK ANSARY
Chairman
Master Mac Limited.
