NOTICE UNDER SECTION 296(2) OF THE COMPANIES ACT, 1994
FOR THE SPECIAL RESOLUTION TO VOLUNTARY WINDING UP

In the matter of
HUDHUD PROKASHON LIMITED

At an Final Extraordinary General Meeting of shareholders of HudHud
Prokashon Limited duly convened and held at the Registered office of the
Company at 3rd Floor, 42/43 North Bruk Hall Road, Bangla Bazar, Dhaka-1204
at 10.00 A.M. on the 30-03-2026 It has been resolved that (1) HudHud
Prokashon Limited (Reg. No. C-196075, Reg. Date: 03-JUL-2024) has been put
into Members voluntary winding up and (2) Md. Shaiful Islam, Managing
Director, Address: Vill. Ramnagar, South Ramchandrapur, Rajfulbaria, Savar,
Dhaka has been appointed as the Liquidator of the Company for the purpose of
voluntary winding up.

AGENDA

(1) Resolved that the final liquidator's report and audited accounts will be
adopted and approved.
(2) Resolved that HudHud Prokashon Limited will finally liquidated and
the liquidator will be discharged from 30-03-2026
(3) Miscellaneous.

All Shareholders are therefore, requested to please attend the meeting in time.

MD. SHAIFUL ISLAM
Liquidator
HudHud Prokashon Limited.
