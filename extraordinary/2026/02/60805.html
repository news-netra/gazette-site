NOTICE OF FINAL EXTRA-ORDINARY GENERAL MEETING FOR
LIQUIDATION
Notice is hereby given to all members that an Extra-Ordinary General Meeting
of SmartCare Solution Ltd. will be held on 30 March 2026 at its registered
office at Union Heights (Level-5), 55-2, Bir Uttam Qazi Nuruzzaman Sarak,
West Panthapath, Dhaka, Dhaka at 10.30 A.M. to transact the following
business and to adopt the following resolutions as Extra-Ordinary General
Meeting.
Agenda to be discussed :
1.
To receive and consider the liquidator's report and accounts prepared by
liquidator;
2.
To wind up the company finally;
3.
To discharge the liquidator; and
4.
Miscellaneous, if any.
All members are requested to attend the meeting.
