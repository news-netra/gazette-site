Notice under section 289 of the companies act 1994
for resolution to wind up voluntarily

In the matter of
BETATECH ENERGY LTD.

An extra ordinary general Meeting of the shareholders of BETATECH
ENERGY LTD. duly convened and held at the registered office of the
Company in Dhaka on 22-02-2026 at 10:00 A.M. It has been resolved that
BETATECH ENERGY LTD. (Reg. No. C-181340/2022 day of 29-05-2022)
has been put into Members for voluntary Winding up on 22-02-2026,
MD MASUM MALLICK, Managing Director BETATECH ENERGY LTD.
Address: HOUSE-21 (Flat B4), Road-21, Sector-11, Uttara Model Town,
Dhaka has been appointed as the Liquidator of the Company with effect from
22-02-2026.

All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the company.

MD MASUM MALLICK
Managing Director
BETATECH ENERGY LTD.
