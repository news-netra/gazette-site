NOTICE
NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289
OF RESOLUTION TO WIND UP VOLUNTARY

In the matter of
KAZI COMPOSIT LTD.

At an extraordinary general meeting of the KAZI COMPOSIT LTD. duly
convened and held at Plot: B-466, B-511, B-512 & B-513, 1st Floor to 4th Floor,
Fatullah, Narayanganj on the 17th February, 2026. It has been resolved that
KAZI COMPOSIT LTD. has been put into Members voluntary winding up as
on 17th February, 2026 and Kazi Sazzad Hossain, Chairman, 56/27, S.M. Maleh
Road (8th Floor), Tanbazar, Narayanganj, has been appointed as the Liquidator
of the Company.

KAZI SAZZAD HOSSAIN
Chairman.
