Date: 17-02-2026
NOTICE TO THE FINAL EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL
MEETING OF AMMANI AGRO & FOODS LTD.
Notice of Final Extraordinary General Meeting UNDER SECTION 296 of the
Companies Act 1994 for Winding-up of AMMANI AGRO & FOODS
LTD. (“the Company”), a Private Limited Company, having Registration No.
C-201313 with the Registrar of Joint Stock Companies & Firms, Dhaka,
Bangladesh, will be held at its registered office at 113/A (3rd Floor), Love Road,
Tejgaon Industrial Area, Dhaka-1208 on 29-03-2026 at 10:00 AM to transact the
following business :
Agenda:
(1) To confirm the minutes of previous meeting ;
(2) To lay the account of the winding-up of AMMANI AGRO & FOODS
LTD. having Registration No. C-201313, showing the manner in which the
winding-up has been conducted and the manner in which the assets of the
Company has been disposed of and provide (if any) explanation by liquidator;
(3) Any other business, if Any.
All Shareholders are therefore, requested to please attend the meeting in time.
On behalf of AMMANI AGRO & FOODS LTD.
MD. ABU MUSA PATWARY
Liquidator & Director.
