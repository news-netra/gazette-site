Date: 01-02-2026
NOTICE
An extraordinary general meeting of the Shareholders of Fair Dairy and Agro
Farms Limited, Reg. No. C-181546 duly convened and held at the Registered
Office of the Company in Dhaka on 22-02-2026 at 10:00 A.M. It has been
resolved that Fair Dairy and Agro Farms Limited, Reg. No. C-181546,
Reg. Date: 08-Jun-2022 has been put into Members for voluntary Winding up
on 22-02-2026 Md. Nurul Amin, Managing Director, Address: Talukder Bari,
Vill-Goverchitra, P.O-Mulpara, Faridganj, Chandpur has been appointed as
the Liquidator of the Company.
All concerned are therefore, requested to contact henceforth with the Liquidator
in respect of any affairs of the Company.
MD. NURUL AMIN
Managing Director.
