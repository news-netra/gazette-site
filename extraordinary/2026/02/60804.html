NOTICE OF FINAL EXTRA-ORDINARY GENERAL MEETING
Notice is hereby given that the Final Extra Ordinary General Meeting of
Shareholders of Starry Enterprise Limited will be held on 31-03-2026 at 11:00
A. M at the Registered Office of the company at Amir Hamza Mir Mansion,
Shimultola, Jamgora, Ashulia, Dhaka, Bangladesh to propose and pass the
following Special Resolution :
1. To receive and consider the liquidator's report and accounts prepared
by him.
2. To transact any other business that the liquidator may place before the
meeting.
3. To publish notice for the final dissolution of the company in Daily
Newspaper as well as the Bangladesh Forms and Publication office.

KAZI MD. MAHBOOB KASEM
(Liquidator).
