NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 OF THE COMPANIES ACT 1994
FOR RESOLUTION TO WIND UP VOLUNTARILY

IN THE MATTER OF
IKD ENGINEERING LTD.

An Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM) of the shareholders of IKD Engineering Ltd.
was duly convened and held at the Registered Office of the Company in Dhaka on 13-05-2026
at 10:00 a.m.

At the said meeting, it has been resolved by the members that the Company, namely
IKD Engineering Ltd. (Registration No. C-197222/2024, Date of Registration:
12 September 2024), be wound up voluntarily as a Members' Voluntary Winding Up with
effect from 13-05-2026, in accordance with the provisions of the Companies Act, 1994.

It has further been resolved that Mr. Shameem Ibrahim, Managing Director of the
Company, having his office at Apt-B204, House 11/B, Road-71, Gulshan-2, Dhaka, be
and is hereby appointed as the Liquidator of the Company with effect from the same
date, for the purpose of conducting the voluntary winding up of the Company.

All concerned persons, authorities, and stakeholders are hereby requested to contact the
Liquidator henceforth in respect of any affairs, claims, or obligations relating to the Company.

Dated: 13-05-2026
