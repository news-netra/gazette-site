NOTICE UNDER SECTION 289 (1) OF THE COMPANIES ACT-1994
RESOLUTION FOR MEMBERS' VOLUNTARY WINDING UP.

In the matter of : DBL BRICKS LIMITED

(Under Members' Voluntary Winding Up)

At an extraordinary general meeting of DBL BRICKS LIMITED duly convened
and held at the Registered/Corporate office of the Company at Suit-4D, South Avenue
Tower, House-50, Road-03, Gulshan-01, Dhaka-1212 on the 17th May, 2026 at
04:00 p.m. It has been resolved that DBL BRICKS LIMITED has been put into
Members' Voluntary Winding Up as on 17th May, 2026 and Mr. Md. Abdul
Jabbar, Address: 102 Green Road, Farmgate, Tajgaon, Dhaka-1215 has been
appointed as the Liquidator of the company with effect from 17th May, 2026.

All concerned are hereby requested to contact henceforth with the liquidator in
respect of any affairs of the company.

MD. ABDUL JABBAR
Managing Director
DBL BRICKS LIMITED
102 Green Road, Farmgate
Tejgaon, Dhaka-1215.
