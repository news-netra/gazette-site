PUBLIC NOTICE
IN THE MATTER OF HATIL LIFESTYLE LTD.
NOTICE OF EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL MEETING UNDER SUB-
SECTION-2 OF SECTION 296 OF THE COMPANIES ACT, 1994
This is to notify that an Extraordinary General Meeting of Hatil Lifestyle Ltd. will be held
on 30-05-2026 at its office at House-35, Road-15, Sector-13, Uttara model Town, Dhaka at
4.00 PM to transect the following special resolution with or without modification:
i. To consider the Liquidators Statement of Financial Affairs on Members' Voluntary
Winding Up of Hatil Lifestyle Ltd.
ii. To Approve the Members' Voluntary Winding Up of Hatil Lifestyle Ltd.
FOR: Hatil Lifestyle Ltd.

Liquidator.
