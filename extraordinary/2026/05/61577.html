NOTICE OF SPECIAL RESOLUTION FOR WINDING UP OF THE
COMPANY VOLUNTARILY AS PER SECTION 289(1) OF THE
COMPANIES ACT 1994
An Extraordinary General Meeting of the shareholders Suddha Pharma Pack
(BD) Limited to be held on 29-04-2026 at 4:30 P.M. in 47/4, Toyenbee Circular
Road, Hatkhola, Dhaka-1203 to pass the following Special resolution.
"Agenda-01: Special resolution for winding up of the Company:
Resolved that as per section 286(1)b of Companies Act 1994 and article 53 of the
Articles of Association, the Company shall be wound up voluntarily. As per
section 292(1) of Companies Act 1994, Dr. Md. Din Islam Miah FCA of 15/5,
Bijoy Nagar, Akram Tower (9th Floor), Dhaka-1000 is appointed as the
Liquidator"
GOPAL CHANDRA MALLICK
Chairman of the meeting.
