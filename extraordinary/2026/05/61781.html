NOTICE OF MEMBERS' VOLUNTARY WINDING-UP

Notice is hereby given that Eximius Automotive Limited, a Private Limited
Company bearing Registration No. C-115425, incorporated on 20 April 14 under
the Registrar of Joint Stock Companies & Firms, Dhaka, Bangladesh, has passed a
Special Resolution in its Extraordinary General Meeting held on 13 May
2026, resolving that the Company be wound up voluntarily as a Members'
Voluntary Winding-up in accordance with the Companies Act, 1994.

Further notice is hereby given that Mr. Kazi Shahnewaj Hossain has been
appointed as the Liquidator of the Company by the members in the said meeting
for the purpose of winding-up the affairs of the Company and distributing its
assets.

KAZI SHAHNEWAJ HOSSAIN
Liquidator
Eximius Automotive Limited.
